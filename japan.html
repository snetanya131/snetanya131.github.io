2. EBISOBA ICHIGEN HOKKAIDO

Got time to waste during your layover? Make sure to tour around Hokkaido-Shinchitose Airport's long row of food! This was one of our favorite dishes, prawn-based soup hokkaido style ramen.








3. SOBADOKORO RAKUICHI NISEKO

Must Try Soba House in Niseko! The place has counter-seating, with an intimate 12-seater capacity. The only menu they serve is Soba Kaiseki, a multi-course meal, in which you pick your choice of fish or meat with a soba dish. Be sure to book your spot way beforehand, as reservations go back up to 2 months.
