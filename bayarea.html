1. NAYA DESSERT BAR

All the sugar, butter, dough lovers--come gather!
Naya is our personal favorite especially after a long day at work. Our go to dessert is the salted egg toast and pork shoulder fried rice. Best place to go when you got nowhere to go!









2. CALZONE'S RESTAURANT

Are we in Italy? Tucked in the busy street of Columbus Ave, Calzone is the perfect people-watching spot! Enjoy a of wine or two while you fantasize your next flight to Naples!











3. KOJA KITCHEN

Our go to place for asian fusion and korean bbq dishes. Thy serve sandwhiches on rice buns and kamikaze fries that are sent from the heavens! You'll leave happy and content as their portions are quite generous.
