1. RUSS & DAUGHTERS

We're at a Lox of Words. It's one of the few family-owned businesses in New York City that holds lots of history. They sell a variety of food from smoked fish, caviar, babka, salad, bagels, and beyond!







2. LE RELAIS DE VENISE

Dinner was the lovely Port Maillot steak frites. ours was cooked medium, just to a slight pink. we asked for more sauce just because the taste resembled something from home. The restaurant is known for its one menu set item conssiting of green walnut salad with mustard vinaugrette, steak frites, and an optional closing dessert. We loved that it tasted like an Indonesian meat dish!





3. TAO DOWNTOWN

We loved the fully-filled potstickers. We ate at TAO out first night in new york city. The dimly lit ambience and majestic asian themed decorations all made our experience enjoyable. The six hungry girls waited 30 minutes to be served food that was later demolished in a matter of 7.
